Introduction Paragrah

Coronavirus (more formally referred to as novel Coronavirus; or COVID-19) is a large family of viruses that has newly been identified in humans. Coronavirus has proven to be a serious threat to the wellbeing of many people across the world. According to CNN of March 2nd, 2020 there have been over 3,000 victims globally. We are interested in researching this field because it is a genuinely concerning illness and there have been several confirmed cases even in North America. As of March 2nd, 2020 there have been 5 deaths due to coronavirus in Washington; this further raises concerns as this issue is now impacting our community directly. So we gather information and datasets from 2019-nCoV Data Repository by Johns Hopkins CSSE and generate this report.

Summary Information

Coronavirus has been reported in many countries, with 75 countries/regions and 141 provinces/states reporting confirmed cases of the virus. The country/region that was most impacted was Mainland China. A total number of 90309 cases have been reported, and of those 45602 have gone on to recover while 3085 have died.
